我的三篇日记

This is three days' worth of a diary from me! Below are my written accounts of November 28, December 02, and December 03 this year!


Monday, November 28
Today I woke up at 7:30.
After I woke up I showered and ate breakfast.
At noon I took the bus to school.
From 2:00-3:30 I did logic class, and after I had a club meeting until 4:45
after which I took the bus home.
Monday, December 02
Today I woke up at 6 and slept again until 8.
I have no class today so I practice Chinese and logic.
After practice, I play games while listening to audiobooks online.
My dad bought a pizza this evening.
My family and I ate it together.
Saturday, December 03
Today, I go shopping with my sister.
She wanted to buy a white jacket.
I didn't want to buy anything.
At noon, I drove her to the MOA.
We walked the MOA for three hours.
She didn't like the coats the stores had.
We ate lunch, and then went home at 5:00 in the evening.
