How do Chinese and American gamers compare?

I made a venn diagram to compare gamers in China to gamers in America.
I'm a PC gamer with a nintendo switch as well, and all of this venn diagram is based purely on
my prior experience as a gamer online.
I hope you find this insightful!

Outside of this, I've got a few other observations.
Firstly, over here there is a conception of Chinese gamers that they are super skilled at any game.
Kind of like, "Americans play for fun without learning the game, and Chinese people have fun by refining their ability to play the game."
There's some times when you are playing in a lobby online in Counter Strike or something and there is a player with chinese characters in their username
that is wiping the whole lobby like they're crumbs on a tablecloth.
In eSports, North America is most dominant in sports games and shooting games.
China is generally excellent internationally for their skill in strategy games.
